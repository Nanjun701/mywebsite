Experimental Camera

Proposal

This project explores how media narratives shape cultural identity. Specifically, it features a camera with three distinct filters, each representing a particular media perspective: negative stereotypes, an authentic yet constantly scrutinized self, and romanticized cultural clich√©s. As viewers move in front of the camera, the filters dynamically change, reflecting these different media portrayals. Through interactive visual effects, users experience firsthand how media narratives can distort or influence perceptions of cultural identity, prompting critical reflection on self-identity and media representation.
