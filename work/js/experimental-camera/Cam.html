Experimental Camera

Description

This is an interactive camera with three filters. Each filter represents a media perspective: a biased reporting perspective, an overly embellished image package, and a seemingly real but always scrutinized “self”. The camera automatically mixes the three filters based on your position in the frame. The more you move, the more a filter “invades” the image. The result is a “you” that is constantly being molded, distorted, and blended.
